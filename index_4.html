// The Founder

Hi, I'm Saharsh

7th Grader · FLL Captain · Future UT Austin Engineer

I'm a 7th grader who started learning to code from zero with one goal: to become a robotics engineer and study at UT Austin. I'm the captain of my First Lego League team, and I built RoboAcademy4Kids because I believe every kid deserves to learn what I'm learning.
